Key Analysis

The dashboard reveals a concerning trend of increasing attack numbers over the years, with a notable surge starting in 2010. The magnitude of this escalation is striking, as the recorded attacks surged from 4,826 in 2010 to a staggering 13,587 in 2016.

Delving deeper, the predominant types of attacks were bombings, armed assaults, and kidnappings, painting a grim picture of the methods employed by terrorist groups. However, what emerges as truly disheartening is that the largest targets of these attacks were private citizens, military personnel, and police forces. One unsettling revelation is that education, which should be a sanctuary of enlightenment, was among the top nine targets.

when examining the data, it is noteworthy that Australia had a relatively lower count of terrorist attacks compared to other regions.
