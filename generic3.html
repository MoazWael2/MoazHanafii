Key Analysis

Analysis Findings:

Churn Rate and Importance: A considerable 26% of total customers churned, highlighting the urgent need to address customer retention as a top priority.

Impact of Relationship Status: Independent or single customers were found to be more likely to churn. This finding calls for targeted efforts to engage and retain this particular customer segment by understanding their unique needs and preferences.

Online Security Usage: An overwhelming 90% of churned customers had phone service but did not use online security features. Emphasizing the importance of online security can help enhance customer trust and loyalty, thereby reducing churn.

Pricing Considerations: Customers who churned after a one-month trial of the fiber optic service cited high costs as a significant factor. Offering flexible pricing options that cater to different customer budgets and preferences can mitigate churn in this regard.

Device Protection: Surprisingly, 64% of churned customers did not have device protection for their phone service. Promoting the benefits of device protection plans, such as device repair or replacement options, can safeguard customers' devices and contribute to an enhanced overall experience.

Online Pickup: Customers who churned were more likely to be using phone service without utilizing the online pickup option. Encouraging customers to utilize online pickup for their convenience and providing a seamless experience can help reduce churn in this area.

Recommendations:

Enhanced Online Security: Invest in robust online security measures and effectively communicate their importance to customers. Assure them of their data privacy and protection, fostering a sense of trust and loyalty.

Device Protection Plans: Educate customers about the benefits of device protection plans, such as device repair or replacement options. Offer affordable and appealing plans to encourage customers to protect their devices. Seamless Online Experience: Improve the online pickup process to provide a convenient and efficient experience. Streamline the process and offer incentives, such as exclusive deals or faster service, to encourage customers to utilize online pickup.
