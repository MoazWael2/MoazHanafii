Key Analysis

In my project, I conducted an in-depth analysis of a struggling food delivery app startup to unlock the secrets to enhancing customer satisfaction. Through thorough examination of their data, I uncovered fascinating correlations that shed light on the root causes of negative reviews and feedback.

By delving into the data, I discovered that delivery persons aged between 35 to 50 years old, particularly those using motorcycles during the night shift, received the most negative reviews and experienced longer delivery times. Furthermore, a strong negative correlation was found between delivery ratings and longer-than-45-minute delivery times.

Building on these insights, I devised actionable recommendations to turn the tide and improve customer satisfaction. These include hiring younger delivery persons for the night shift, preferably under 35 years old, and assigning alternative modes of transportation like bicycles or scooters to navigate high-traffic areas during late hours. By implementing these changes, the startup can optimize their operations and reduce negative reviews.

Through the power of data analysis, we can transform the fortunes of the food delivery app startup, revolutionize customer satisfaction, and pave the way for a thriving business in the highly competitive food delivery industry.
